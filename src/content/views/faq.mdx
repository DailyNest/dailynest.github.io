### What is DailyNest?

DailyNest is a content-first publication covering technology, programming, productivity, lifestyle, and more.

### How can I contribute an article?

Fork the repository on GitHub, add your article under `src/content/articles`, and open a pull request.

### Can I use external images in articles?

Yes. You can provide a thumbnail URL in the frontmatter and embed images directly in the Markdown content.

### Where can I find the RSS feed?

The RSS feed is available at `/rss.xml`.
