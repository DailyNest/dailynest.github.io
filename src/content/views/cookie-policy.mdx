# Cookie Policy

DailyNest uses cookies and similar technologies to improve site functionality, personalize content, and analyze traffic.

Common cookie uses on this site:

- Session management (to keep you logged in to optional services)
- Analytics (to understand how visitors use the site)
- UI preferences (dark mode, presentation choices)

You can control cookies through your browser settings. If you have questions or want specific cookie information, contact the maintainer at [contact@muhammadfiaz.com](mailto:contact@muhammadfiaz.com).
